Grammarly - Writing Assistant

Grammarly is an AI-powered writing assistant that helps improve grammar, spelling, clarity, and tone in real-time. Whether you're working on emails, essays, or creative writing, Grammarly boosts your writing confidence and effectiveness.

✨ Key Features:

  • Real-time grammar and spelling suggestions
  • Tone detection and clarity improvements
  • Plagiarism checker (Premium)
  • Works across browsers, MS Office, Google Docs, and more
