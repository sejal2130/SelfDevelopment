Anki - Flashcards

Anki is a powerful and customizable flashcard tool based on spaced repetition. It’s ideal for memorizing anything from vocabulary to complex medical or coding concepts.

✨ Key Features:

  • Spaced Repetition for efficient memorization
  • Supports images, audio, video, and LaTeX
  • Free and open-source
  • Available on Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS
